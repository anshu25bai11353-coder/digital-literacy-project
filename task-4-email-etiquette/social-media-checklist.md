# Social Media Do's & Don'ts for College Students

---

## DO's ✅

1. **Keep profiles professional** - Use a clear profile picture and write a bio that reflects your goals

2. **Think before posting** - Ask yourself: Would I show this to my parents or a future employer?

3. **Use privacy settings** - Control who can see your posts and personal information

4. **Connect with professionals** - Follow industry leaders, professors, and companies in your field

5. **Showcase achievements** - Share projects, certifications, hackathons, and academic accomplishments

6. **Be respectful** - Engage in positive conversations and avoid arguments

7. **Verify information** - Check facts before sharing news or articles

8. **Secure your accounts** - Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication

---

## DON'TS ❌

1. **Don't share personal info** - Never post your phone number, address, or financial details

2. **Don't post inappropriate content** - No offensive language, hate speech, or controversial topics

3. **Don't engage with trolls** - Ignore negative comments and block abusive accounts

4. **Don't overshare** - Avoid posting every moment of your life

5. **Don't accept unknown requests** - Only connect with people you know or trust

6. **Don't post during class** - Stay focused on academics

7. **Don't share OTPs or passwords** - Never share login credentials with anyone

8. **Don't believe everything** - Be aware of fake news and misinformation

---

## Reporting Channels

If you face cyberbullying or online harassment:
- **Report** to the platform immediately
- **Take screenshots** as evidence
- **Block** the user
- **Inform** parents or college authorities

---

**Remember:** Your digital footprint stays with you forever. Post wisely!
